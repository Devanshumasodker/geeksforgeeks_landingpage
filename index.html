GeeksforGeeks

What is GeeksforGeeks?

GeeksforGeeks is a computer science portal for geeks. It contains computer science and programming articles, quizzes, and practice problems.

What is the purpose of GeeksforGeeks?

The main purpose of GeeksforGeeks is to help students and professionals learn computer science and programming easily. It is a platform to learn and practice computer science and programming problems.

What is the vision of GeeksforGeeks?

The vision of GeeksforGeeks is to provide free computer science and programming resources to help students learn computer science easily and find job opportunities in IT companies.
